        <h3 style="border:0px; margin:8px 0px; padding:0px; font-size:1.4em; color:rgb(51,51,51); font-family:&quot;Open Sans&quot;,&quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,STHeiti,&quot;Microsoft Yahei&quot;,sans-serif">
            合法的集合名</h3>
        <ul style="border:0px; margin:1em 0px; padding:0px; line-height:16.8px; list-style-type:none; color:rgb(51,51,51); font-family:&quot;Open Sans&quot;,&quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,STHeiti,&quot;Microsoft Yahei&quot;,sans-serif">
            <li style="border:0px; margin:0px 0px 1em 1em; padding:0px 0px 0px 1.5em; line-height:1.5em; font-size:1em">
                集合名不能是空字符串&quot;&quot;。</li><li style="border:0px; margin:0px 0px 1em 1em; padding:0px 0px 0px 1.5em; line-height:1.5em; font-size:1em">
                集合名不能含有\0字符（空字符)，这个字符表示集合名的结尾。</li><li style="border:0px; margin:0px 0px 1em 1em; padding:0px 0px 0px 1.5em; line-height:1.5em; font-size:1em">
                集合名不能以&quot;system.&quot;开头，这是为系统集合保留的前缀。</li><li style="border:0px; margin:0px 0px 1em 1em; padding:0px 0px 0px 1.5em; line-height:1.5em; font-size:1em">
                用户创建的集合名字不能含有保留字符。有些驱动程序的确支持在集合名里面包含，这是因为某些系统生成的集合中包含该字符。除非你要访问这种系统创建的集合，否则千万不要在名字里出现$。　</li></ul>
        <p style="border:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; line-height:2em; font-family:&quot;Microsoft Yahei&quot;,&quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif; color:rgb(51,51,51)">
            如下实例：</p>

            capped collections</h3>
        <p style="border:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; line-height:2em; font-family:&quot;Microsoft Yahei&quot;,&quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif; color:rgb(51,51,51)">
            Capped collections 就是固定大小的collection。</p>
        <p style="border:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; line-height:2em; font-family:&quot;Microsoft Yahei&quot;,&quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif; color:rgb(51,51,51)">
            它有很高的性能以及队列过期的特性(过期按照插入的顺序). 有点和 &quot;RRD&quot; 概念类&#20284;。</p>
        <p style="border:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; line-height:2em; font-family:&quot;Microsoft Yahei&quot;,&quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif; color:rgb(51,51,51)">
            Capped collections是高性能自动的维护对象的插入顺序。它非常适合类&#20284;记录日志的功能 和标准的collection不同，你必须要显式的创建一个capped collection， 指定一个collection的大小，单位是字节。collection的数据存储空间&#20540;提前分配的。</p>
        <p style="border:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; line-height:2em; font-family:&quot;Microsoft Yahei&quot;,&quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif; color:rgb(51,51,51)">
        </p>
        <span style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-family:&quot;Open Sans&quot;,&quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,STHeiti,&quot;Microsoft Yahei&quot;,sans-serif">要注意的是指定的存储大小包含了数据库的头信息。</span>
        <p style="border:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; line-height:2em; font-family:&quot;Microsoft Yahei&quot;,&quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif; color:rgb(51,51,51)">
        </p>
        <pre class="prettyprint prettyprinted" style="border-width:1px 1px 1px 4px; border-style:solid; border-color:rgb(221,221,221); margin:15px auto; padding:10px 15px; line-height:20px; font-family:&quot;courier new&quot;; white-space:pre-wrap; word-break:break-all; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(51,51,51)"><span class="pln" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(0,0,0)">db</span><span class="pun" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(102,102,0)">.</span><span class="pln" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(0,0,0)">createCollection</span><span class="pun" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(102,102,0)">(</span><span class="str" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(0,136,0)">&quot;mycoll&quot;</span><span class="pun" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(102,102,0)">,</span><span class="pln" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(0,0,0)"> </span><span class="pun" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(102,102,0)">{</span><span class="pln" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(0,0,0)">capped</span><span class="pun" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(102,102,0)">:</span><span class="kwd" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(0,0,136)">true</span><span class="pun" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(102,102,0)">,</span><span class="pln" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(0,0,0)"> size</span><span class="pun" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(102,102,0)">:</span><span class="lit" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(0,102,102)">100000</span><span class="pun" style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px; color:rgb(102,102,0)">})</span></pre>

            <li style="border:0px; margin:0px 0px 1em 1em; padding:0px 0px 0px 1.5em; line-height:1.5em; font-size:1em">
                在capped collection中，你能添加新的对象。</li><li style="border:0px; margin:0px 0px 1em 1em; padding:0px 0px 0px 1.5em; line-height:1.5em; font-size:1em">
                能进行更新，然而，对象不会增加存储空间。如果增加，更新就会失败 。</li><li style="border:0px; margin:0px 0px 1em 1em; padding:0px 0px 0px 1.5em; line-height:1.5em; font-size:1em">
                数据库不允许进行删除。使用drop()方法删除collection所有的行。</li><li style="border:0px; margin:0px 0px 1em 1em; padding:0px 0px 0px 1.5em; line-height:1.5em; font-size:1em">

                在32bit机器中，capped collection最大存储为1e9( 1X10<sup style="border:0px; margin:0px; padding:0px">9</sup>)个字节。</li></ul>
